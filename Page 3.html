Stepney City Farm

Date: Tuesday 2nd October

Location: Stepney Way, Stepney Green, London, E1 3DG

Stepney City Farm is a three-acre working farm who give children and adults a chance to meet farm animals, learn how to grow food and try out arts and crafts. It's an educational charity working to improve lives through farming. The farm provides a welcoming green space to bring together a diverse community, and cultivate wellbeing through high-welfare, environmentally sustainable farming practices. Learn more here.
