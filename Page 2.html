South London Cares

Date: Tuesday 2nd October

Location: The British Home, Crown Lane, London, SW16 3JB

A charity community network which aims to combat social isolation and loneliness in Southwark and Lambeth. It runs social clubs for people over 65 and young professional volunteers. The clubs take the form of special outings and visits, which are a great opportunity for the older neighbours to experience new things in their community, which they may not have the confidence to visit alone.
